Never say NO to Adobo

Adobo is a traditional Filipino dish that is loved for its savory and tangy flavors. This recipe features chicken thighs marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, then simmered until tender. Serve it with steamed rice for a comforting meal.

Adobo chicken has been my family's go-to dish for years and I hope that you will enjoy it as much as we do.
